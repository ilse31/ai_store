# Product Overview

**AI Store** is a premium e-commerce platform for AI tools and services, designed with a BMW M-inspired design language. The platform features a catalog of AI products (ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, etc.) with multiple pricing tiers, a shopping cart system, and WhatsApp-based order processing.

## Core Features

- **Product Catalog**: Browse AI tools organized by category (chatbot, image-generation, productivity)
- **Multi-Variant Products**: Each product offers multiple subscription tiers (1 month, 3 months, lifetime, etc.)
- **Shopping Cart**: Add multiple products/variants, review orders, and proceed to checkout
- **Order Processing**: Checkout flow collects buyer information and generates invoice for WhatsApp-based order completion
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach with BMW M-inspired dark theme and premium aesthetics

## Target Audience

Indonesian market seeking premium AI tool subscriptions with local payment methods and WhatsApp-based customer support.

## Design Philosophy

The platform adopts BMW M's design language: near-pure black canvas, white typography, sharp rectangular components (no rounded corners except circular icon buttons), uppercase labels with letter-spacing, and full-bleed imagery. The aesthetic emphasizes "engineered precision" over consumer-tech softness.
